On the wax tablet

δέλτος — the Greek wax tablet. One smooth surface you write anything onto, then warm, smooth, and reuse. Nothing precious about any single page; the value is in the surface that is always ready.

One substrate, many surfaces.

That is the whole idea. The notes are not the app — they are what passes through it. Underneath, a single store everything reads and writes, so a list today and a map tomorrow are the same notes, seen differently.
